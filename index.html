About Us

The Early Childhood Foundation of Cabarrus County (ECFCC) envisions high-quality and well-supported preschool programs for every child in Cabarrus County.

Party on the Patio - June 7th, 2025

Join us on June 7th for the “Party on the Patio" hosted by the Early Childhood of Cabarrus County Foundation! Enjoy great food, one alcoholic beverage, a DJ, and exciting silent auction items. All proceeds support underfunded preschools, bringing essential arts classes to preschool-aged children in our community. We are also seeking sponsors at various levels. Please reach out if you're interested in supporting this great cause!

Mission Monday - June 23rd, 2025

Join us on June 23rd from 4-10pm for “Mission Monday" at Cabarrus Brewing Company! A portion of the sales on Mission Monday will help support our mission to provide high-quality preschool programs for every child in Cabarrus County Come for the fun, stay for the cause! Plus, you can round up all month long to contribute!

Our Mission

To develop and support educational programs and advocate for pre-school aged economically disadvantaged children by building partnerships within the community.

Why?

To invest in the future of our community by providing educational opportunities for preschool age children who qualify for a subsidized education.
